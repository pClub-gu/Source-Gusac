Our Motto !

“It’s not what you know; it’s what you do with what you know”

Our primary objective is to integrate knowledge and transfer the same to students. GUSAC provides an exclusive platform to the "Budding Future" by nourishing their creativity. It will unleash the genius within them and capacitate them to implement their ideas in numerous problems arising in the world.

Our Ideology

What is GUSAC ?
GUSAC - GITAM University Science and Activity Centre. The GUSAC team is privileged to invite the students across the GITAM University. After hutting in continuous several untiring efforts we have been successful in launching a Science and Activity Centre at GITAM University.A fresher aims to attain his/her goals during his/her tenure in the university. GUSAC provides scope for students like these by imparting knowledge through classes, friendly seminars, practical assignments; it also enables them to build a team for participating in fests and assists them with the required infrastructure to make their projects, besides creating a platform for the student research.
What will I learn ?
It creates an environment for interested students to evenly space out and implement their ideas. Many students who have potential and are interested in participating in various events fail to do the same, mostly due to lack of proper inter-departmental knowledge and freedom to put forth their ideas. This Centre fills these gaps and provides better prospects for the students of GU. Students are exposed to national level competitions and are provided with a platform to put forth their projects and to gain recognition for their work. Most of the projects aimed at will be targeted towards nation-building, to help the needy and the disabled.
Stepping Stones To Success
A student gathering takes place every week to discuss interests and recent trends in management, science and technology. Students work on their projects and ideas with freedom and utilities. Experienced students give friendly seminars and problem statements are given to students as practical assignments to enhance their engineering skills. Events like expos, seminars, fests and workshops are conducted on a regular basis and representatives from the industry as well as academic circles like IIT are invited to these events. Faculty members who are interested in sharing their knowledge are always be welcome to do so in any of the aforementioned events and gatherings.

History
GUSAC is a dream come true for many students of GITAM University. GUSAC organised its inaugural function on 22nd July 2011 with the start of a new academic year and with the aim of imparting knowledge to all through different means. The team GUSAC follows a motto “ It’s not what you know, it’s what you do with what you know ”. The motto which inspires each and every student to think beyond their course modules and reach heights in the world of empirical engineering and sciences through hard work and determination.

The Various Clubs of the SAC

  • Tech-Club
  • G-Studio
  • G-Mag
  • Literary Club
  • Creative Arts
  • Programming Club
The Technical Club constitutes sectors of the SAC responsible for project execution, development of a working model or new innovations that are being implemented by the Club. We take a project through the stages from requirement gathering to deployment of the final product.
G-STUDIO is a platform for students in GITAM University to showcase their talents in photography and videography. Creativity is precious, it is our birthright and a glowing light that resides within each one of us, making us special and unique. We at G-STUDIO provide you with limitless possibilities and support to satisfy your creative needs and provide the help needed to improve your skills and learn things everyday.
Our magazine is bimonthly. Each issue has sections for developments in GITAM, technological advancements, articles on famous personalities and student/students of the week. The entertainment section has: movie reviews, a movie bucket list, pages of my diary, comic strips, game machine and more. Our magazine caters to a wide audience as it is a student magazine and students tend to have a variety of interests. We can assure you that anyone who reads the magazine will find it intriguing. We are proud to say that G-Mag is AP's first online student magazine.
The Literary club is the propagation and promulation of the development of communication and soft skills among students, a platform for the crafting of a Literary culture of formal and informal writing and an oppurtuntiy for the competitive interaction of the University.
As much as science and technology forms a part of the student's life, the cultural and creative aspects are also important.And that's why, this club forms an integral part of the SAC.A blend of creative thinking with technical innovations is the motto !
